Product Requirements Document (PRD): ACP
=====================================

Vision
------
Make autonomous AI agents safe to deploy at enterprise scale — without requiring enterprises to choose a single model, framework, or cloud provider.

Product Goals
-------------

G1 — Open Standard: Publish the Agent Identity Specification (AIS) as open-source, establishing ACP as the industry standard for agent identity before incumbents define the category.

G2 — Full-Stack Control: Be the only platform covering all seven governance primitives in a single, integrated control plane. No competitor owns more than two.

G3 — Framework Neutrality: Support all major agent frameworks from day one. Never require enterprises to change their agent stack to adopt ACP.

G4 — Developer-Led Adoption: Start with a free-tier SDK developers adopt bottom-up; convert to enterprise contracts when security teams discover fleet usage.

G5 — Regulatory Readiness: Be the authoritative compliance infrastructure for EU AI Act, SOC 2, HIPAA, and FedRAMP-aligned AI deployments.
